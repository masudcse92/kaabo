KAABO WOLF WARRIOR X DUAL MOTOR ELECTRIC SCOOTER

This escooter is a powerhouse with dual 1100W motors and a top speed of 70 km/h. Its dual suspension system, hydraulic front fork, and forged metal frame provide a smooth ride. The TFT display and adjustable controllers allow for customized acceleration, while front LED lights and adjustable side/logo lights provide visibility and atmosphere. HYDRAULIC SUSPENSION SYSTEM

Hydraulic suspension uses dampers to absorb shock and vibration, delivering a stable and comfortable ride on bumpy terrain. This technology ensures reduced fatigue and stress on the rider's body, making for a comfortable and stable ride, even during extended trips.

RIDE MORE. CHARGE LESS

The Wolf Warrior X e-scooter features a premium LG/Samsung battery for long rides with a maximum range of 110 km. Durable and reliable, this battery provides peace of mind for any adventure.

PORTABILITY AND PERFORMANCE

The Wolf Warrior X electric scooter has a durable forged frame that can withstand daily use. Its compact and foldable design offers convenient transportation and storage, making it an ideal choice for high-performance riders who value both durability and portability.

How fast can the Kaabo scooter go?

The speed of Kaabo scooters depends on the model. Our Wolf King GT can reach speeds up to 60 mph, while others may have lower maximum speeds.

What is the range of the Kaabo scooter on a single charge?

The range of Kaabo scooters also varies depending on the model and conditions. Generally, Kaabo scooters can travel between 15-50 miles on a single charge, depending on factors such as rider weight, terrain, and speed.

Is my Kaabo electric scooter waterproof?

Kaabo recommends all riders to maintain safe riding habits. We strongly recommend that you do not ride your Kaabo E-Scooter in rainy or snowy weather. In order to protect the quality and functionality of your Kaabo E-Scooter, avoid wet environments and weather.

What is the weight limit for the Kaabo scooter?

The weight limit for Kaabo scooters varies depending on the model. Generally, Kaabo scooters can support riders up to 220-330 pounds, depending on the model. It's important to check the specific weight limit for the Kaabo scooter you are interested in before purchasing.
