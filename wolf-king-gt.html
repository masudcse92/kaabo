WOLF KING GT ULTRA PERFORMANCE OFF-ROAD ELECTRIC SCOOTER

The Wolf King GT electric scooter: Ultra performance off-road solution with 2x2000W dual motors, 180 km range, 100 km/h top speed, and LG/Samsung 72V 35Ah battery with heat flow technology. Winner of ESG 2022-23 Speed Champion award.

HYDRAULIC SUSPENSION SYSTEM 110 STEERING

The hydraulic suspension system with dampers absorbs shock and vibration, providing a stable and comfortable ride even on uneven terrain. The front fork design allows for a 110 turning angle, resulting in less fatigue for the rider on long rides.

HYDRAULIC SUSPENSION SYSTEM 110 STEERING

The Wolf King GT e-scooter features a premium LG/Samsung battery for long rides with a maximum range of 180 km. Durable and reliable, this battery provides peace of mind for any adventure especially with its waterproofed charging port mechanism.

PORTABILITY AND PERFORMANCE

Made of aviation-grade aluminum alloy, the Wolf King GT's sturdy frame can handle daily use and support up to 150 Kg.Despite its powerful motors and large battery, the foldable design allows for easy storage and transportation, making it a versatile and convenient choice for performance-seeking riders.
