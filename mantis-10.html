KAABO MANTIS 10 DUAL MOTOR ELECTRIC SCOOTER

The Kaabo Mantis 10 Dual Motor Electric Scooter offers unbeatable speed and adventure. With dual 1000W motors and 10-inch tires, it provides power for daily commuting and off-road riding. The lightweight frame and dual shocks ensure a smooth ride, while the battery with BMS offers a long range. Enjoy the ultimate ride with the Mantis 10 today!

Dual Shocks and 10' Pneumatic Tires

The Mantis 10 electric scooter is equipped with dual shocks and 10" pneumatic tires, providing a smooth and comfortable ride no matter the terrain. The dual shocks absorb the impact of bumps and rough surfaces, while the large 10" tires provide enhanced stability and traction. Whether you're cruising on city streets or exploring off-road trails, the Kaabo Mantis 10 will take you there in style and comfort.

High Energy Battery with BMS

The Mantis 10 electric scooter is equipped with a high-energy lithium-ion battery that features 78 units of high-density 18650 cells, providing long-lasting power for your ride. With this advanced battery technology, the Mantis 10 can reach maximum speeds and travel long distances on a single charge. The smart battery management system (BMS) ensures the health of the battery by monitoring its performance and protecting it from overcharging, over-discharging, and short circuits.

Lightweight and Foldable

The Mantis 10 electric scooter is designed with a lightweight aviation-grade 6061/6082 aluminum alloy frame and weighs only 25 kg. The frame is forged from a single piece, making it not only light but also very durable. Aluminum is an excellent choice for a commuter scooter because it is much stiffer than steel and provides a smooth ride. In addition, the frame of the Mantis 8 is foldable, making it convenient to store and transport.

Kaabo recommends all riders to maintain safe riding habits. We strongly recommend that you do not ride your Kaabo E-Scooter in rainy or snowy weather. In order to protect the quality and functionality of your Kaabo E-Scooter, avoid wet environments and weather.
